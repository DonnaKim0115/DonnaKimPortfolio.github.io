Compliment Card Render


What?
A 3D Render of a "Compliment Card" made in Blender as a Student Council initiative.

Each year as a Student Council tradition for End of Bullying Week, Compliment Cards are distributed to students to write positive messages to be delivered to fellow students and create a welcoming school environment. Due to COVID-19 restrictions and safety procautions in 2021, as acting Deputy Prime Minister of Student Council at the time, virtual Compliment Cards were taken on as a project.

To advertise the use of virtual Compliment Cards to the student body and to utilize my 3D rendering skills, I created a 3D render of the Compliment Cards in Blender to use in the social media campaign which I produced in Adobe Premiere Pro, as shown below:
