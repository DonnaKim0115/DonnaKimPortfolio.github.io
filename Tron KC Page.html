SOLIDWORKS Keychain Render

What?
A keychain model made in SOLIDWORKS as a project for the MTE100 course. The render is a replica of the keychain all Mechatronics students make in the UWaterloo Student Machine Shop (as shown above). Measurements for the model are to scale to the real keychain and so are the materials. From modelling the pieces to a full free moving assembly, the project incases all main features of SOLIDWORKS.
