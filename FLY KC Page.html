3D Printed 2-Component Keychain

What?
A 2 component keychain modelled in Fusion360 and 3D printed. To incorperate colours within the keychain on a single extrusion 3D printer, 2 components were modelled to be press fitted together. Once colour was added (through painting) on the keychain base, the top component of keychain was press-fitted to the base to create a multi-coloured keychain.
