1P5S Battery Pack Module


What?
A 1P5S (1 parallel, 5 series), 20-volt battery module made for the University of Waterloo's Midnight Sun design teams intro project.

How Does it Work?
The project uses 5 4-volt cells connected in series, held together using a 3D printed top/bottom plate designed in Fusion360. The batteries are connected using nickel plates spot-welded between negative and positive terminals.

Skills Applied
  • Cell voltage testing
  • 3D modelling
  • 3D printing
  • Spot-welding
